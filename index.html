Leading the construction industry in Kenya with unwavering commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Our Story

Founded in 2008, Stontech General Supplies and Construction Limited has grown from a small local contractor to one of Kenya's most trusted construction companies. Our journey began with a simple vision: to deliver exceptional construction services that exceed client expectations while maintaining the highest standards of quality and professionalism.

Over the years, we have successfully completed over 500 projects ranging from residential homes to large commercial complexes, earning the trust of clients across Kenya through our commitment to excellence, innovation, and sustainable building practices.
